## What this is · 这是什么

A **meta-ontological adjudication** of the boundary of neuroscience. Not a critique of the discipline — the opposite gesture: **granting neuroscience the strongest ontological claim it can honestly make** (`I = brain`), and on that premise, marking with precision what lies outside the reach of that claim without diminishing the field.

一份对神经科学学科边界的**元本体论裁决**。不是对学科的批判——而是相反的姿态：**授予神经科学它能诚实说出的最强本体论命题**（`我 = 大脑`），并以此为前提，精确标出此命题不可触及的射程，而不削弱学科。

This is *not* a theory of consciousness. It does not compete with GWT, IIT, HOT, RPT, or PP. It clarifies the syntactic premises under which any such theory becomes possible.
